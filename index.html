Introducing Bali Kelod

Located far from the bustling tourist hubs of southern Bali, Buleleng in Bali Utara offers a different side of the island—one that is serene, unspoiled, and filled with natural wonders. Buleleng is known for its breathtaking landscapes, including dramatic coastlines, lush hillsides, and hidden waterfalls. It's a region where nature, culture, and tranquility blend harmoniously, offering a peaceful retreat for those who wish to escape the crowds and connect with the essence of Bali.

If you're a food lover, Buleleng District in Bali is a treasure trove waiting to be explored. From the serene shores of Lovina Beach to the vibrant streets of Singaraja, this region is home to a diverse array of dining spots that showcase the rich flavors of Asian cuisine.

4.4 (900 Reviews)

Nasi Bakar SOS, Sukasada

4.6 (700+ Reviews)

Nasi Bakar SOS is a popular eatery in Sukasada, known for its delicious and unique take on traditional Indonesian cuisine. The restaurant specializes in Nasi Bakar, which is rice wrapped in banana leaves and grilled to perfection, infusing it with a smoky aroma. They offer a variety of fillings, including chicken, seafood, and vegetarian options, all paired with flavorful sambals and fresh herbs.
