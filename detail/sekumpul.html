Sekumpul Waterfall: Nature’s Hidden Gem

Sekumpul Village, Sawan District, Buleleng Regency

4.5 (1380+ Reviews)

Open At 6.00 AM - 6.00 PM Local Time

About

Located in the lush, tropical landscape of Sekumpul village, Sekumpul Waterfall is one of Bali’s most stunning natural attractions, offering both adventure and tranquility. Situated in the northern part of the island, near Lemukih village in Buleleng, this waterfall is a hidden gem, less crowded than other popular spots, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers seeking peace and thrill-seekers alike.

Sekumpul Waterfall is renowned for its height - approximately 80 meters (262 feet)—and its unique multi-tiered cascades, where the water splits into several streams before plunging into the refreshing natural pool below. The sound of water crashing against the rocks, combined with the lush greenery surrounding it, creates an atmosphere of serenity and awe.

Highlights

  • Explore the seven waterfalls of Sekumpul, including the main cascade
  • Adventure more safely with an experienced guide and safety gear
  • Capture stunning photos on a private tour: just you and your group

What To Expect

What sets Sekumpul apart from other waterfalls in Bali is not just its beauty but the adventure it offers:


  • Scenic Trekking: The journey to Sekumpul Waterfall involves a scenic trek through rice terraces, local villages, and lush forests, offering a glimpse into Bali’s natural beauty and local culture.
  • Multiple Waterfalls: Visitors can explore several smaller waterfalls in the area, each with its own unique charm and beauty, making it a paradise for nature enthusiasts and photographers.
  • Natural Pools: For those seeking a refreshing dip, the natural pools at the base of the waterfalls provide a perfect spot to cool off and relax amidst the stunning scenery.

Why Visit Sekumpul Waterfall?

Whether you’re seeking a peaceful escape into nature or looking for an action-packed adventure, Sekumpul Waterfall offers the best of both worlds. It’s an off-the-beaten-path destination that allows you to experience Bali’s natural beauty in its rawest form. Plus, with its options for trekking, exploring multiple waterfalls, and swimming in natural pools, it’s the perfect spot for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike.
