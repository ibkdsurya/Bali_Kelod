Banjar Hot Spring: A Relaxing Thermal Oasis

Banjar Village, Banjar District, Buleleng Regency

4.3 (3470+ Reviews)

Open At 8.30 AM - 5.30 PM Local Time

About

Located in the serene village of Banjar, near Lovina in the northern part of Bali, Banjar Hot Spring is a tranquil retreat known for its therapeutic hot springs. Surrounded by lush tropical gardens, this natural hot spring offers a peaceful escape where visitors can relax and rejuvenate in the warm, mineral-rich waters. It’s an ideal destination for those seeking relaxation and wellness.

Banjar Hot Spring features several pools with varying temperatures, allowing visitors to choose their preferred level of warmth. The water, which is believed to have healing properties, flows from a natural spring and is channeled through stone-carved spouts into the pools. The serene atmosphere and the soothing effect of the hot springs make it a perfect spot for unwinding and enjoying the natural beauty of Bali.

Highlights

  • Relax in the therapeutic hot spring pools
  • Enjoy the lush tropical surroundings and tranquil atmosphere
  • Experience the traditional Balinese architecture and stone carvings

What To Expect

What sets Banjar Hot Spring apart from other destinations in Bali is its relaxing environment and therapeutic benefits:


  • Therapeutic Waters: The mineral-rich waters of Banjar Hot Spring are believed to have healing properties, making it a popular spot for those seeking relief from various ailments and stress.
  • Multiple Pools: The hot spring features several pools with different temperatures, allowing visitors to choose their preferred level of warmth and enjoy a soothing soak.
  • Scenic Surroundings: The hot spring is set amidst lush tropical gardens, providing a serene and picturesque environment for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Why Visit Banjar Hot Spring?

Whether you’re seeking a therapeutic soak or a peaceful escape into nature, Banjar Hot Spring offers a unique and relaxing experience. It’s an off-the-beaten-path destination that allows you to enjoy the natural beauty of Bali in a tranquil setting. With its options for soaking in therapeutic waters, enjoying the lush surroundings, and experiencing traditional Balinese architecture, it’s the perfect spot for those looking to unwind and rejuvenate.
