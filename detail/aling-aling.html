4.5 (354 Reviews)

About

Nestled in the lush, tropical landscape of Sambangan village, Aling-Aling Waterfall is one of Bali’s most breathtaking natural wonders, offering both adventure and serenity. Located in the northern part of the island, near Sing Sing village in Buleleng, this waterfall is a hidden gem, less crowded than other well-known spots, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers seeking tranquility and thrill-seekers alike.

Aling-Aling Waterfall stands out for its height - approximately 35 meters (115 feet)—and its unique twin cascades, where the water splits into two separate streams before plunging into the refreshing natural pool below. The sound of water crashing against the rocks, combined with the lush greenery surrounding it, creates an atmosphere of peace and awe.

What To Expect

What sets Aling-Aling apart from other waterfalls in Bali is not just its beauty but the adventure it offers:


  • Natural Water Slides: For those seeking a rush, the surrounding area features several smaller waterfalls that have smooth rocks, creating natural water slides where you can glide directly into the pools below.
  • Cliff Jumping: Visitors can also test their bravery by cliff jumping from heights ranging between 5 to 15 meters into the crystal-clear waters. It’s an adrenaline-pumping experience that attracts adventurers from all over the world.
  • Natural Water Slides: For those seeking a rush, the surrounding area features several smaller waterfalls that have smooth rocks, creating natural water slides where you can glide directly into the pools below.

Why Visit Aling-Aling Waterfall?

Whether you’re seeking a peaceful escape into nature or looking for an action-packed adventure, Aling-Aling Waterfall offers the best of both worlds. It’s an off-the-beaten-path destination that allows you to experience Bali’s natural beauty in its rawest form. Plus, with its options for trekking, sliding, and jumping, it’s the perfect spot for adrenaline junkies looking for fun and excitement amidst Bali’s scenic landscapes.
