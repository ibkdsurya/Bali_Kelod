About

Located on the northern coast of Bali, Lovina Beach is a serene and picturesque destination known for its calm waters, black sand beaches, and vibrant marine life. Situated near the town of Singaraja in Buleleng, this beach is a hidden gem, offering a peaceful retreat away from the more crowded southern beaches. It’s an ideal destination for those seeking relaxation and a slower pace of life.

Lovina Beach is famous for its dolphin sightings, where visitors can embark on early morning boat tours to watch these playful creatures in their natural habitat. The beach’s calm waters also make it perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and diving, providing opportunities to explore the rich underwater world of Bali’s northern coast.

Highlights

  • Experience early morning dolphin watching tours
  • Enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and diving in calm waters
  • Relax on the unique black sand beaches

What To Expect

What sets Lovina Beach apart from other beaches in Bali is its tranquil environment and unique experiences:


  • Dolphin Watching: One of the main attractions of Lovina Beach is the early morning dolphin tours, where visitors can see pods of dolphins swimming and playing in the open sea.
  • Water Activities: The calm waters of Lovina Beach are perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and diving, offering a chance to explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Black Sand Beaches: The unique black sand of Lovina Beach, created by volcanic activity, provides a stunning contrast to the clear blue waters and is perfect for a relaxing day by the sea.

Why Visit Lovina Beach?

Whether you’re seeking a peaceful escape by the sea or looking to explore Bali’s marine life, Lovina Beach offers a unique and tranquil experience. It’s an off-the-beaten-path destination that allows you to enjoy the island’s natural beauty in a serene setting. With its options for dolphin watching, water activities, and relaxing on black sand beaches, it’s the perfect spot for those looking to unwind and connect with nature.
